Refund Policy

Thank you for purchasing our course, enrolling for our workshop (offline or online) and/or subscribing to our services operated under RSBI or Learn with RSBI.

  1. Once purchased, our courses, workshops and/or services cannot be cancelled and are non-refundable.
  2. You may however choose to join a different batch (subject to the availability of courses or workshops).
  3. You may not let another candidate attend the course or workshop instead of you.
  4. The candidate is solely responsible for inability to attend any course or workshop due to any reason whatsoever that falls beyond the purview of RSBI (eg. Student unable to attend due to Power outage or natural calamity or acts of God, etc.)
  5. The completion certificate will only be issued once and in the name of the candidate registering and attending the complete course or workshop on attending the course/workshop or after passing the required examination or completing the given assignment (whichever is applicable).
  6. If you have any additional questions or would like to request a batch change or candidate name transfer, feel free to contact us on the respective support emails mentioned in the FAQ section of the course or workshop.
  7. The request for batch change or candidate name transfer should be made at least 48 hours before the start of the course or workshop, post which you will not be eligible for any changes in batch or candidate name.
  8. If a candidate fails to attend the course or workshop, he/she will be marked absent and no extended access will be provided over the mentioned validity of modules/lessons. The candidate is also not eligible for a refund under such conditions.
  9. Any batch transfers, refunds or cancellations are at the discretion of RSBI.
  10. For any technical issues that you face, the single point of contact will be the respective mail mentioned on the webpage of Learn with RSBI. The time of response shall be a minimum of 2 working days.
  11. Any legal matters, if they prop up, shall only be undertaken in metropolitan courts in Thane, Maharashtra.
